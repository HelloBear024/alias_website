Privacy Policy for Alias - Guess Multilingual

This Privacy Policy explains how Alias ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and safeguards your information when you use our mobile application, Alias - Guess Multilingual. We are committed to providing a fun, offline-first party game experience.


1. Information We Collect

Game Data: Team names, scores, game settings, and dictionaries are stored locally on your device to enable gameplay. We do not transmit this data to external servers.

Device Data: We may collect non-personal information such as device type and operating system version for app stability and crash reporting via third-party tools (like Firebase).


2. Online & Offline Use

Alias - Guess Multilingual is primarily an offline game. No internet connection is required to play the core game. Internet access may be required for downloading new dictionaries or languages updates if available.


3. Advertising & Analytics

We use third-party advertising services (e.g., Google AdMob) to display ads. These services may collect unique device identifiers or use cookies to serve personalized ads based on your interests. We also use analytics tools to understand which languages and categories are most popular to improve future updates.

  • Google Privacy Policy

4. Data Security

We prioritize the security of your data. As most gameplay data is stored locally on your device, it remains in your control. For any data processed by third-party ad networks, standard industry encryption and security protocols are used.


5. Your Privacy Rights

You can manage your data preferences within the app settings. You may also reset your advertising ID in your device settings to limit ad tracking. You can delete local game history by clearing the app data or uninstalling the application.


6. Children’s Privacy

Alias is a party game intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from children under 13.


7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may revise this policy periodically. Updates appear on this page with the revision date. Continued use indicates acceptance.
